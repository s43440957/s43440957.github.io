Locals is an online community web application that groups people based on the location/suburb that they live in.

Features

Login/Register
Login, Register, Forgot Password, and Logout.
Google map API
Google Map which can be zoomed in or out to find a specific area.
Post News
Users can post the news that they see in the surrounding area.
News Library
The news are sorted in the descending order of number of views. User can click on any of the news in the list to view its detail.
Transparent circle searching area
Transparent circles displayed on the Google Map shows visually the news’ region contour, the larger the circle is, the more news are contained in the region.
About Us
It displays the information of the development team including pictures of each developer, our names, and the tasks that we have done during the development process.
Leave comments
Comments towards each news can be left by the users. A title is required to be entered, body of comment can be purely text or pictures can be attached. Users get to choose whether to upload the current location when the comments were posted.
