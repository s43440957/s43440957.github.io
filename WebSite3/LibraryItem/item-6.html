Chindera

Originally the gross tonnage listed in this database for the 'Chindera' was 11 tons.

However, Stone (2006:172) lists the tonnage of the vessel to be either 186 or 111 and that 11 could be a typo. This is supported by the Marine Board Inquiry findings in The Sydney Morning Herald (29/09/1896:3) which lists the vessel as 111 tons. Dimensions taken from Stone (2006:172).
