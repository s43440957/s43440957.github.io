Clayton

The 'Clayton' was wrecked on a reef off Port Kennedy, Torres Strait, in July 1914 (Stone 2006:185).

The 'Clayton' was originally listed as wrecked off Thursday Island in this database, however, according to Stone (2006:185), while the 'Clayton' was originally reported to have been wrecked off Thursday Island in April 1814, the vessel was actually wrecked off Port Kennedy.Vessel dimensions taken from Stone (2006:185).
